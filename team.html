Olesea Bortniac

An interdisciplinary artist using macro lenses, prisms, ferrous liquids, vibration, light, and generative digital visualizations to experiment with the representation of scientific concepts associated with natural phenomena. She creates new patterns and structures which often look like live organisms. Her work explores the intersections between science, technology and ecology, using speculative design methods.

Nicholas Faris

A sound designer and artist from South Wales, currently based in London. Nicholas works with spatialised sound and composition, constructed and manipulated from sounds and rhythms captured from our surrounding sonic environment. Inspired by his curiosity and fascination with sound, Nicholas informs his practice with research that traverses the connections between modes of listening and lived experiences. Since graduating from the Sound Design programme at the RCA in 2022, Nicholas has been taking work to exhibitions and performances internationally and within the UK
